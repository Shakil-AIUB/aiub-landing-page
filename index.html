About AIUB

Our History & Mission

Established in 1994, American International University-Bangladesh (AIUB) has emerged as one of the leading private universities in Bangladesh. Founded by Dr. Anwarul Abedin, AIUB is committed to providing quality education to create future leaders.

Our mission is to foster the intellectual and personal growth of students through excellence in teaching, research, and service. We strive to create an environment that encourages critical thinking, creativity, and lifelong learning.
